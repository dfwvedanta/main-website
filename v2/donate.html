Make a Difference

The Ramakrishna Vedanta Society of North Texas is a non-profit organization dedicated to spreading the timeless teachings of Vedanta and serving the community through spiritual education and charitable activities.

All our programs and services are offered free of charge. We rely entirely on the generous donations of our members and well-wishers to sustain our activities and maintain our center.

All tax deductible donations to support the Ramakrishna Vedanta Society of North Texas are gratefully accepted. All donations are tax-deductible under Section 501(c)(3) of the IRS code.

What Your Donation Supports

Spiritual Programs

Weekly meditation sessions, study circles, and spiritual discourses led by qualified teachers and monastics.

Guest Speakers

Hosting visiting swamis and scholars from the Ramakrishna Order to lead retreats and special programs.

Facility Maintenance

Upkeep of our chapel, library, and meeting spaces to provide a peaceful environment for spiritual practice.

Library Resources

Maintaining and expanding our collection of spiritual books and literature for community members.

Children's Programs

Educational activities teaching spiritual values and Indian culture to the next generation.

Community Service

Food drives and charitable activities serving those in need, following Swami Vivekananda's ideal of service.

Thank You

Your generosity enables us to continue our mission of spiritual education and service to humanity. Every donation, regardless of size, makes a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.

"Service to humanity is service to God" - Swami Vivekananda
