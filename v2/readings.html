Our Library Collection

Our library houses an extensive collection of spiritual books and literature, covering Vedanta philosophy, meditation, yoga, and the teachings of the great spiritual masters. Members and visitors are welcome to browse and borrow books for their spiritual studies.

The collection includes works by and about Sri Ramakrishna, Holy Mother Sarada Devi, Swami Vivekananda, and other direct disciples, as well as books on Hindu scriptures, comparative religion, and practical spirituality.

Visiting Our Library

The library is open during our regular Sunday services (11:00 AM - 12:30 PM) and by appointment. Books may be borrowed for up to two weeks, with renewals available.

We also maintain a selection of books available for purchase at nominal cost. All proceeds go toward expanding our library collection and supporting our programs.

For more information about library access or to inquire about specific books, please contact us.
