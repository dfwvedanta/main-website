Guidelines for Visitors

What to expect when visiting our center

Arrive Early

Please arrive 5-10 minutes before the scheduled time for seating and settling in.

Respectful Attire

Modest and comfortable clothing is recommended. Please remove shoes before entering the chapel.

Silence Phones

Please silence all electronic devices to maintain a peaceful atmosphere.

Children Welcome

Families with young children are welcome. We have a separate children's program available.

Accessible Parking

Ample parking is available. Accessible parking spots are located near the entrance.

Questions Welcome

Feel free to ask questions after the program. Our community is here to support your journey.
