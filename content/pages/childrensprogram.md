## Building Character Through Spiritual Values

Our Children's Program aims to instill spiritual and moral values in young children through stories, songs, activities, and interactive lessons. We help children develop character, compassion, and an understanding of their cultural and spiritual heritage.


Classes are conducted in a warm, loving environment where children learn about the great spiritual teachers of India, practice meditation and yoga, and develop qualities such as truthfulness, kindness, courage, and respect for all.

## Class Schedule

Our Children's Program runs concurrently with the Sunday service, allowing parents to attend the adult program while children participate in age-appropriate activities.




### Sunday Classes

Every Sunday | 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM


Classes are divided by age groups to ensure appropriate content and engagement for all children.



**Age Groups:**



- Younger Children (Ages 5-8)

- Older Children (Ages 9-12)

- Teens (Ages 13+)

## Join Our Program

We welcome all children to participate in our program. There is no registration fee, and all materials are provided. Parents are encouraged to support their children's spiritual education by reinforcing the values and lessons learned at home.


For more information about our Children's Program or to register your child, please contact us or visit us on Sunday.
