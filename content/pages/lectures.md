## Our YouTube Channel

Watch inspiring lectures, spiritual discourses, and teachings from our Sunday services and special programs. Our YouTube channel features talks on Vedanta philosophy, meditation techniques, and the teachings of Sri Ramakrishna, Holy Mother Sarada Devi, and Swami Vivekananda.







                            Visit Our YouTube Channel

## Join Our Sunday Services

Many of our lectures are recorded during our Sunday services. We welcome you to join us in person every Sunday at 11:00 AM for meditation and spiritual discourse.


Can't attend in person? Selected lectures are uploaded to our YouTube channel so you can watch from anywhere in the world.
