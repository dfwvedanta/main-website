## Our Location



## Parking Information

Ample free parking is available on the premises. Please park in the designated areas and be mindful of neighbors' driveways and property.


During busy Sunday services and special events, additional parking may be available on the street. Please observe all posted parking regulations.


If you have mobility concerns or require accessible parking, please contact us in advance so we can ensure appropriate accommodation.

## Visitor Guidelines

**Dress Code:** We encourage modest, respectful attire. Traditional Indian clothing is welcome but not required.


**Footwear:** Please remove your shoes before entering the chapel and meditation areas. Shoe racks are provided near the entrance.


**Punctuality:** Sunday services begin promptly at 11:00 AM with silent meditation. We encourage arriving a few minutes early to settle in.


**Mobile Devices:** Please silence all phones and electronic devices before entering the meditation hall.


**Children:** Children are always welcome. A dedicated Children's Program runs during Sunday services for ages 5 and up.


**First-Time Visitors:** Please feel free to introduce yourself to anyone! We're happy to answer questions and help you feel at home.
