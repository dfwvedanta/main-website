## Have a Question?

Whether you're curious about Vedanta philosophy, meditation practices, our programs, or spiritual life in general, we welcome your questions. Our community members and teachers are here to help guide you on your spiritual journey.

Please fill out the form below with your email address and your question. We'll respond as soon as possible.

### Common Questions

**What is Vedanta?**

Vedanta is the philosophical foundation of Hinduism, based on the ancient Upanishads. It teaches the ultimate unity of individual soul with the Universal Soul. [Learn more about Vedanta](vedanta.html).

**Do I need to be Hindu to attend your programs?**

Not at all! Vedanta is universal in its approach and welcomes people of all faiths, backgrounds, and beliefs. Our doors are open to anyone seeking spiritual growth and understanding.

**Are your programs suitable for beginners?**

Yes! We offer programs for all levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Our [meditation classes](meditation.html) and [lectures](lectures.html) are accessible to everyone.

**How can I support your organization?**

We appreciate your support! You can [donate online](donate.html), volunteer at our events, or simply attend our programs and spread the word about Vedanta.
