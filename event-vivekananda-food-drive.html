Vivekananda Food Drive 2026

Serving Our Community with Compassion

Date: January 18-25, 2026 Time: All Week (9:00 AM - 5:00 PM daily) Location: Ramakrishna Vedanta Society of North Texas

About This Food Drive

In honor of National Vivekananda Youth Day (January 12) and continuing the spirit of service throughout the week, we are organizing our annual Vivekananda Food Drive. This community service initiative embodies Swami Vivekananda's timeless teaching: "Service to man is service to God."

This year's food drive aims to collect non-perishable food items, personal care products, and essential supplies for local families in need. All donations will be distributed to partnering food banks and community organizations serving the Dallas-Fort Worth area.

Whether you contribute a single item or organize a collection drive, every donation makes a meaningful difference in someone's life. Join us in this noble cause and experience the joy of selfless service.

What We're Collecting

Most Needed Items:

  • Canned Goods: Vegetables, fruits, soups, beans, tuna, chicken
  • Dry Goods: Rice, pasta, cereal, oatmeal, flour, sugar
  • Protein Sources: Peanut butter, canned meats, dried beans, nuts
  • Pantry Staples: Cooking oil, spices, salt, condiments
  • Personal Care: Toothpaste, soap, shampoo, deodorant, feminine hygiene products
  • Baby Supplies: Diapers, baby food, formula, wipes
  • Household Items: Paper towels, toilet paper, cleaning supplies

Please Note: All items must be unexpired and in original packaging. We cannot accept opened or homemade food items.

How to Participate

Drop-Off Schedule:

  • Monday - Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Sunday: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM (after Sunday service)

Ways to Contribute:

  1. Drop Off Donations: Bring items to our center during the collection week
  2. Organize a Collection Drive: Gather donations from your neighborhood, workplace, or school
  3. Monetary Donations: Financial contributions will be used to purchase needed items in bulk
  4. Volunteer: Help sort, pack, and distribute donations (sign up required)

Volunteer Opportunities:

We need volunteers to help with:

  • Receiving and sorting donations
  • Packing food boxes for distribution
  • Loading and transporting items to partner organizations
  • Administrative support and coordination

Our Impact

Since 2015, our annual Vivekananda Food Drive has collected and distributed over 25,000 pounds of food to families in need across North Texas. Last year alone, we served more than 150 families and partnered with 5 local organizations to maximize our community impact.

This year, we aim to exceed these numbers and expand our reach to serve even more families facing food insecurity. Your participation makes this possible.
