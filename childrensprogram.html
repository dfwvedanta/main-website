Children's Program

Nurturing young minds and hearts

Building Character Through Spiritual Values

Our Children's Program aims to instill spiritual and moral values in young children through stories, songs, activities, and interactive lessons. We help children develop character, compassion, and an understanding of their cultural and spiritual heritage.

Classes are conducted in a warm, loving environment where children learn about the great spiritual teachers of India, practice meditation and yoga, and develop qualities such as truthfulness, kindness, courage, and respect for all.

What We Teach

Spiritual Stories

Tales from Hindu mythology, stories of Sri Ramakrishna, Swami Vivekananda, and other spiritual luminaries.

Moral Values

Teaching universal values like truthfulness, compassion, courage, self-control, and respect through stories and activities.

Meditation & Prayer

Simple meditation techniques, breathing exercises, and devotional prayers appropriate for children.

Yoga & Movement

Basic yoga postures and movements that help children develop physical health and mental focus.

Cultural Heritage

Introduction to Indian culture, festivals, and traditions that celebrate spirituality and family values.

Creative Activities

Arts, crafts, music, and games that reinforce spiritual lessons in fun and engaging ways.

Class Schedule

Our Children's Program runs concurrently with the Sunday service, allowing parents to attend the adult program while children participate in age-appropriate activities.

Classes are divided by age groups to ensure appropriate content and engagement for all children.

Age Groups:

  • Younger Children (Ages 5-8)
  • Older Children (Ages 9-12)
  • Teens (Ages 13+)

Special Activities

Festival Celebrations

Children participate in celebrating major Hindu festivals with special programs, plays, and activities.

Summer Programs

Extended summer activities including day camps, special workshops, and cultural programs.

Service Projects

Age-appropriate community service activities teaching the value of serving others.

Parent-Child Events

Special programs where parents and children learn and practice spirituality together.

Join Our Program

We welcome all children to participate in our program. There is no registration fee, and all materials are provided. Parents are encouraged to support their children's spiritual education by reinforcing the values and lessons learned at home.

For more information about our Children's Program or to register your child, please contact us or visit us on Sunday.
