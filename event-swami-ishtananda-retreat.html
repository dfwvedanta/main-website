About This Retreat

We are delighted to welcome Swami Ishtananda for a transformative weekend retreat that promises to be a deeply enriching spiritual experience. This special retreat will offer participants an opportunity to immerse themselves in the profound teachings of Vedanta philosophy and engage in intensive meditation practices.

Over the course of two days, Swami Ishtananda will deliver inspiring talks on key aspects of Vedantic philosophy, including the nature of the Self, the path to self-realization, and the practical application of Vedanta in daily life. Each session will be complemented by guided meditation sessions designed to help participants deepen their spiritual practice.

About Swami Ishtananda

Swami Ishtananda is a senior monk of the Ramakrishna Order, known for his deep spiritual insights and ability to communicate complex philosophical concepts in an accessible manner. With over two decades of monastic life dedicated to the practice and teaching of Vedanta, he has inspired countless seekers on their spiritual journeys.

His talks are characterized by their clarity, depth, and practical wisdom, drawing from both ancient scriptures and personal realization. Swami Ishtananda's gentle demeanor and profound knowledge create an atmosphere conducive to spiritual growth and self-inquiry.
