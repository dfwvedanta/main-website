Driving Directions

From Dallas (via I-35E)

  1. Take I-35E South toward Fort Worth
  2. Exit at TX-183 West / Airport Freeway
  3. Take exit for Story Road
  4. Turn right on Story Road
  5. Turn left on Scotland Drive
  6. Destination will be on your left at 119 W. Scotland Drive

From Fort Worth (via TX-183)

  1. Take TX-183 East / Airport Freeway
  2. Take exit for Story Road
  3. Turn left on Story Road
  4. Turn left on Scotland Drive
  5. Destination will be on your left at 119 W. Scotland Drive

From DFW Airport

  1. Head east toward TX-183
  2. Merge onto TX-183 East / Airport Freeway
  3. Take exit for Story Road
  4. Turn left on Story Road
  5. Turn left on Scotland Drive
  6. Destination will be on your left at 119 W. Scotland Drive

Approximately 10-15 minutes from the airport

Parking Information

Free parking is available on the premises. Please use the driveway and parking area in front of and beside the building. During busy times (such as Sunday services and special events), additional street parking is available on Scotland Drive.

Please be mindful of our neighbors and avoid blocking driveways or mailboxes when parking on the street.

Additional Information

Building Access: The main entrance is at the front of the building. During scheduled programs, the door will be unlocked. For other times, please contact us in advance to arrange access.

Accessibility: The facility is wheelchair accessible. Please contact us if you have specific accessibility needs so we can ensure your comfort during your visit.

First Time Visitors: Don't worry if you arrive a few minutes late to a program. Simply enter quietly and take a seat. After the program, please feel free to introduce yourself to our ministers or other attendees. Everyone is welcome!
